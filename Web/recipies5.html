5.Spaghetti with Clams & Garlic

Ingredients:

8 oz spaghetti
2 tablespoons olive oil
4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust to taste)
1 cup dry white wine
1 lb fresh clams, scrubbed and cleaned
Salt and black pepper to taste
2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
Grated Parmesan cheese for serving (optional)

Cooking Instructions:

1. **Cook the Spaghetti:** - Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2. **Sauté Garlic and Red Pepper Flakes:** - In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. - Add thinly sliced garlic and red pepper flakes. Sauté for 1-2 minutes until the garlic is fragrant but not browned.

3. **Cook Clams:** - Pour in the dry white wine and bring it to a simmer. - Add cleaned clams to the pan, cover, and cook for 5-7 minutes or until the clams open. - Discard any unopened clams.

4. **Combine Spaghetti and Clams:** - Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan with the clams. - Toss everything together until the pasta is well coated in the sauce.

5. **Season and Garnish:** - Season with salt and black pepper to taste. - Sprinkle chopped fresh parsley over the pasta and toss again.

6. **Serve:** - Divide the spaghetti and clams among serving plates. - Optionally, serve with grated Parmesan cheese on top. - Enjoy your delicious Spaghetti with Clams & Garlic!
