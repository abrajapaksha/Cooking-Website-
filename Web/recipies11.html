11.Pumpkin Ice Cream

Ingredients:

1 cup canned pumpkin puree
1 cup whole milk
2 cups heavy cream
3/4 cup granulated sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
1/4 teaspoon salt

Cooking Instructions:

1. **Prepare Pumpkin Base:** - In a blender, combine pumpkin puree, whole milk, heavy cream, granulated sugar, vanilla extract, ground cinnamon, ground ginger, ground nutmeg, and salt.

2. **Blend and Chill:** - Blend the ingredients until smooth. - Chill the mixture in the refrigerator for at least 4 hours or overnight.

3. **Churn Ice Cream:** - Pour the chilled pumpkin mixture into an ice cream maker. - Churn according to the manufacturer's instructions until it reaches a soft-serve consistency.

4. **Freeze:** - Transfer the churned ice cream to a lidded container and freeze for an additional 4 hours or until firm.

5. **Serve and Enjoy:** - Scoop the Pumpkin Ice Cream into bowls or cones. - Garnish with a sprinkle of cinnamon if desired, and enjoy the creamy pumpkin goodness!
