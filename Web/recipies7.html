7.Perfect Hamburger

Ingredients:

1 lb ground beef (80% lean)
Salt and black pepper to taste
4 hamburger buns
4 slices of cheddar cheese
Lettuce leaves
Tomato slices
Red onion slices
Pickles
Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise for condiments

Cooking Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Patties:** - Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and shape each into a patty. - Season both sides of the patties with salt and black pepper.

2. **Cook the Patties:** - Preheat a grill or stovetop pan over medium-high heat. - Cook the patties for 4-5 minutes per side for medium doneness, adjusting the time based on your preference. - Place a slice of cheddar cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking, allowing it to melt.

3. **Assemble the Burgers:** - Toast the hamburger buns on the grill or in a toaster. - On the bottom half of each bun, place a lettuce leaf, a cooked patty with melted cheese, tomato slices, red onion slices, and pickles. - Spread ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise on the top half of the buns.

4. **Serve:** - Place the top half of the bun over the assembled ingredients to create the perfect hamburger. - Serve immediately and enjoy with your favorite side dishes!
