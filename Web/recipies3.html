3.Spaghetti Crispy ZucchiniPasta

Ingredients:

2 medium-sized zucchinis
8 oz spaghetti
2 tablespoons olive oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
Salt and black pepper to taste
Grated Parmesan cheese for garnish
Fresh basil leaves for garnish (optional)

Cooking Instructions:


1. Spiralize the zucchinis to create zucchini noodles. Set aside.

2. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

3. In a large pan, heat olive oil over medium heat. Add minced garlic and red pepper flakes (if using). Sauté for about 1 minute until fragrant.

4. Add zucchini noodles to the pan and toss for 2-3 minutes until they are just tender. Season with salt and black pepper to taste.

5. Add the cooked spaghetti to the pan and toss everything together until well combined and heated through.

6. Serve the Spaghetti Crispy Zucchini Pasta in bowls, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese and fresh basil leaves if desired.
