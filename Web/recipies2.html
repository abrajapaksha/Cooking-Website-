2.Instant Pot Bourbon Chicken

Ingredients:

500g boneless chicken, cut into cubes
1/2 cup soy sauce
1/2 cup brown sugar
1/4 cup bourbon
1/4 cup ketchup
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 teaspoon ginger, grated
1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
1 tablespoon cornstarch
2 tablespoons water
Green onions for garnish (optional)
Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Cooking Instructions:


1. In a bowl, whisk together soy sauce, brown sugar, bourbon, ketchup, minced garlic, grated ginger, and red pepper flakes (if using).

2. Add chicken cubes to the Instant Pot, then pour the sauce over the chicken.

3. Close the Instant Pot lid, set the valve to "Sealing," and cook on high pressure for 8 minutes.

4. Once cooking is complete, perform a quick pressure release.

5. In a small bowl, mix cornstarch with water to create a slurry. Stir the slurry into the Instant Pot to thicken the sauce.

6. Serve the Instant Pot Bourbon Chicken over rice, garnished with green onions and sesame seeds if desired.
