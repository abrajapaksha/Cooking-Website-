6.Cajun Seafood Boil

Ingredients:

4 lbs large shrimp, shell-on
2 lbs crawfish, cleaned
2 lbs snow crab legs
1 lb Andouille sausage, sliced
6 ears corn on the cob, halved
2 lbs red potatoes, halved
1 cup unsalted butter
1/2 cup Cajun seasoning (adjust to taste)
1 lemon, sliced
Fresh parsley for garnish

Cooking Instructions:

1. **Prepare Seafood and Vegetables:** - Rinse shrimp, crawfish, and crab legs under cold water. Pat them dry. - In a large pot, combine seafood, Andouille sausage, corn, and potatoes.

2. **Make Cajun Butter Sauce:** - In a saucepan, melt unsalted butter over medium heat. - Stir in Cajun seasoning and cook for 2-3 minutes until fragrant. - Pour the Cajun butter sauce over the seafood and vegetables in the pot.

3. **Boil Seafood:** - Fill the pot with enough water to cover the seafood and vegetables. - Bring the water to a boil and cook for 10-15 minutes until the seafood is fully cooked and the potatoes are tender.

4. **Serve:** - Drain the seafood and vegetables. - Transfer to a large serving platter or spread on a covered table. - Squeeze fresh lemon over the seafood, and garnish with chopped fresh parsley.

5. **Enjoy:** - Dig in and enjoy the flavorful Cajun Seafood Boil with your favorite dipping sauces!
