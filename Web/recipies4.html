4.15 Minute Lo Mein images

Ingredients:

8 oz lo mein noodles
2 tablespoons sesame oil
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 tablespoon oyster sauce
1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
1 teaspoon sugar
2 tablespoons vegetable oil
2 cloves garlic, minced
1 cup sliced mushrooms
1 cup shredded carrots
1 cup snow peas, ends trimmed
1 cup shredded cabbage
1/2 cup sliced green onions
Sesame seeds for garnish (optional)

Cooking Instructions:


1. Cook the lo mein noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.

2. In a small bowl, whisk together sesame oil, soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and sugar. Set aside.

3. Heat vegetable oil in a large pan or wok over medium-high heat. Add minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

4. Add mushrooms, shredded carrots, snow peas, and shredded cabbage to the pan. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes until the vegetables are tender-crisp.

5. Add the cooked lo mein noodles and the sauce to the pan. Toss everything together until well combined and heated through.

6. Garnish with sliced green onions and sesame seeds if desired. Serve the 15-Minute Lo Mein hot and enjoy!
