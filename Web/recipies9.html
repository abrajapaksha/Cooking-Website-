9.No-Bake Avocado Cheesecake

Ingredients:

2 ripe avocados
250g cream cheese
1/2 cup sugar
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup heavy cream
1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
1/2 cup unsalted butter, melted
Lime zest for garnish

Cooking Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Crust:** - In a food processor, combine graham cracker crumbs and melted butter. - Press the mixture into the base of a springform pan to create the crust.

2. **Blend Avocado Mixture:** - In a blender, blend avocados, cream cheese, sugar, and vanilla extract until smooth.

3. **Whip the Cream:** - In a separate bowl, whip the heavy cream until stiff peaks form. - Gently fold the avocado mixture into the whipped cream until well combined.

4. **Assemble and Refrigerate:** - Pour the avocado mixture over the crust in the springform pan, spreading it evenly. - Refrigerate the cheesecake for at least 4 hours or overnight to set.

5. **Garnish and Serve:** - Before serving, garnish with lime zest for a refreshing touch. - Slice and enjoy your delightful No-Bake Avocado Cheesecake!
